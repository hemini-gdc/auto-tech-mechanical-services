Our Specialist Services

Full Service

A comprehensive inspection and maintenance service covering all major components of your vehicle, including fluids, filters, brakes, suspension, tyres, belts, and overall performance.

Interim Service

A lighter, cost‑effective service between major services with essentials like oil and filter changes, fluid top‑ups, and a general safety inspection.

Vehicle Scanning & Diagnosis

Advanced computer diagnostics to identify engine faults, warning lights, electrical issues, and performance problems quickly and accurately.

Batteries & Tyres

Supply and installation of quality batteries and tyres, plus tyre balancing, puncture repairs, and battery testing for reliable motoring.

Oil Replacement

Quick and efficient engine oil and filter changes using the correct grade to improve performance, reduce wear, and extend engine life.

Cam Belts

Professional cam belt inspection and replacement to prevent major engine damage, following manufacturer guidelines for correct timing and long‑term reliability.

Brake Servicing & Repairs

Complete brake system checks, pad and rotor replacements, brake fluid changes, and repairs to keep your braking safe, responsive, and road‑worthy.

Shock Replacement

Replacement of worn shock absorbers to improve ride comfort, handling, and vehicle stability for safer braking and cornering.

Suspension

Inspection and repair of suspension components including springs, control arms, bushings, and struts to restore smooth driving and proper alignment.

CV Joints

Repair or replacement of CV joints and boots to eliminate clicking noises, vibrations, and prevent drivetrain damage.

W.O.F Repairs

Fast, reliable repairs to help your vehicle pass its Warrant of Fitness, fixing common issues with lights, brakes, suspension, tyres, and structure.

WINZ Quote

Preparation of detailed Work and Income New Zealand quotes so eligible customers can access approved repair funding before work begins.
