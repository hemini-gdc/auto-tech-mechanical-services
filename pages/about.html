About Us

Auto Tech Mechanical Services Huntly is an independent automotive repair service proudly based in Huntly, New Zealand. As a one-person workshop, every job is completed with a strong focus on quality, honesty, and attention to detail.

With years of hands-on mechanical experience, the goal is simple: provide reliable repairs, honest advice, and fair pricing. Customers deal directly with the person doing the work, ensuring clear communication and quality workmanship every time. The goal is to provide you with fair pricing and reliable mechanical services without any hassle.

From servicing and brake repairs to diagnostics and general maintenance, Auto Tech Mechanical Services Huntly is dedicated to help keep your vehicle running at its best. Supporting the community and delivering dependable service is at the heart of everything we do.
